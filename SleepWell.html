Our Story

What is SleepWell Spray?

SleepWell Spray is a student-run wellness business offering a line of natural sprays designed to ease stress, improve sleep, and support everyday hygiene and freshness.

Born from the everyday struggles of university life — exams, late nights, shared rooms — our products bring calm and cleanliness to students wherever they are, anytime they need it.

Every product is compact, affordable, and refillable, helping students save money while reducing waste. Because everyone deserves to live well.
